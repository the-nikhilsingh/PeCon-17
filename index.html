Letter from the Secretary General

Greetings!

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you all to the second edition of The People’s Conference to be held during the 11th to the 13th of March at PES University, Bangalore. The People’s Conference, born in mid-2015 is slowly rising up to be one of the more famous Model UN conferences in South India. In its 2015 edition, “PECON” (as it is commonly called) saw some wonderful debate, generated multiple good reviews and had the opportunity to host a stellar Executive Board; all of which we aim to positively triple in this second edition. With some brilliant (and different) committees, amazing amenities and a secretariat with a collective experience of more than 175 MUNs, I can safely promise you a brilliant conference. On behalf of the entire secretariat, I can assure you that we will do our level best in giving you your time’s worth of three days. As my last Model UN as an organizer, I urge you to come and be a part of this phenomenon; we assure you that it will leave a brilliant and lasting impression.PES has always inculcated anything related to academic excellence and with the continuous support of the Institution, this academic simulation will definitely be a rolling success. It is an honour and indeed a privilege to serve as the Secretary General of The People’s Conference for the second time in a row and lead such a brilliant and dedicated team to host all you amazing delegates and Executive Board members in what should be THE MUN.

Come, and be a part of three days of intense debate combined with some brilliant food and incredible hospitality; come and be a part of The People’s Conference, 2016. One. Last. Time. Cheers!


 Yash Vijay,
Secretary General,
The People’s Conference, 2016.
